How Prism Works

A three-layer pipeline that turns tribal knowledge into structured, queryable intelligence.

Step 01

Connect & Ingest

OAuth into GitHub, Slack, and docs. Prism runs a continuous AST parser and diff watcher across your entire codebase in minutes.

Step 02

Build the Graph

Neo4j maps every function, module, author, and thread into a living knowledge graph that tracks relationships, deprecations, and ownership.

Step 03

Ask Anything

GPT-4 + LangChain agents answer codebase questions with precise citations — linking code, commits, Slack threads, and document sources instantly.
